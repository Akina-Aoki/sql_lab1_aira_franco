---
title: Sakila Database Analysis
---

This analysis supports management decisions for a DVD rental business to increase revenue.

The goal is to understand what drives revenue in different aspects, how pricing and content length affect performance, and where inventory and demand are misaligned.


### Contents
- SQL analysis using DuckDB and Pandas DataFrames
- Reproducible transformations (Joins, CTEs, window functions)
- Metrics related to revenue, genres, ranking segments, runtime, and other customer behaviors
- Visuals generated directly from query results
